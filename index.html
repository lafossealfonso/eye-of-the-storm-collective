The ever-changing climate presents a unique opportunity for those fascinated by atmospheric science. Eye of the Storm Collective is a dedicated community of meteorology enthusiasts, storm trackers, and extreme weather observers committed to studying, documenting, and understanding the complexities of regional weather systems.

Our Mission

We provide a platform for individuals passionate about meteorology, severe weather phenomena, and climate science. Through real-time data collection, field observation, and expert discussions, we aim to advance weather awareness while fostering a network of informed weather trackers.

Collective's Areas of Focus

  • Severe Weather Tracking & Analysis
  • Meteorological Research & Local Forecasting
  • Atmospheric Photography & Documentation
  • Field Expeditions & Storm Chasing

Get Involved

Eye of the Storm Collective welcomes professionals, amateur meteorologists, photographers, and anyone intrigued by the science of weather. Membership offers access to forecasting workshops, collaborative research projects, and organized storm observation outings.

Join us in the pursuit of meteorological discovery. Sign up today and become part of a leading community for extreme weather observation.
